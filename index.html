WHO WE ARE

After a 2016 mission trip to Tenwek, Kenya, Amalin Sorajja and Andrew Yang founded OR Supply to supply overseas hospitals that lack equipment. Beginning with balloon valvuloplasties, a remedy to rheumatic mitral valve damage, OR Supply aims to train and supply doctors in impoverished areas with lifesaving surgeries.

SUPPLYING

Supplying donated equipment to operating rooms in need. All equipment is sterilized, shipped, and tracked(see map below) at overseas hospitals. Now you know when your donation has made a difference.

TRAINING

Training regional doctors and surgeons in procedures for long term development and impact.

Balloon Valvuloplasty

Rheumatic Heart Disease(RHD) is widespread in African countries, such as Kenya, due to a lack of antibiotics. RHD can be a residual effect of strep throat, malnorishment, congenital heart disease, and/or poor healthcare. RHD causes the heart's valves to shrink and tighten.

Shrunken aortic or/and mitral valve(s) cause regurgitation and a high pressure of blood flow in the heart. This interferes with blood supply to the body which makes daily tasks a struggle. of people struggle with RHD. Unfortunately, without proper procedure, recovery from RHD is difficult.

Dr. Paul Sorajja performed balloon valvuloplasties to treat RHD. The balloon valvuloplasty is a minimally invasive surgery using a catheter and wire to enter the heart. A balloon catheter then inflates to widen the damaged heart valve and reverse symptoms of RHD. In the future, OR Supply hopes to train and supply local doctors in Africa to combat RHD.
